Poetry

Most of my writing takes place in my Notes app. Below is one that I'm willing to put on the internet :) It's about the tender, unspoken relationships that emerge out of routine, and their fleeting nature.

to the barista who would start my order
as soon as she saw me come in

we didn't chat but one time i complimented
your new hair and you blushed

i don't know your name but i always hoped
you enjoyed your job or at least got along
with your coworkers

the day before i moved to a different
neighborhood i realized that i probably
would never come to your coffee shop again

but we didn't chat so i didn't tell you and i
tipped you 20% and i left
